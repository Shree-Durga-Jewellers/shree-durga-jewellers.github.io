About The Shop:

Established in 1968, Shree Durga Jewellers, was started in Power House Camp-II, Bhilai by Mr. Mahavir Prasad Verma.
With over 50+ years in the business the organisation has established a strong foothold in the Industry.
Thier Moto of believing in customer satisfaction while maintaining the highest quality of the product comes to the utmost importance to them.

Product and Services offered:

Shree Durga Jewellers is one of the leading business in Gold, Silver and Diamond jewellery.
We is also known for its precious stone collection, fashion and latest and the most trending jewellery designs, fancy jewellery, Immitation jewellery, Kundan Jewellery, Silver Utensils, etc.
We also has a wide range of South Indian jewellery and apart from the ready made jewellery, It also provides the service of custom making jewellery in either Gold or Silver with high precision and maintaining the highest quality.
Every jewellery either from the collection or custom made is ensured to be with a HallMark, HUID marked and with proper certificates and bills accordingly.

The staff at the establishment are courteous and prompt at providing any assistance. They are also readily available to answer any queries or questions you may have.
